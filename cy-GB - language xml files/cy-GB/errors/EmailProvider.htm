Critical Error: emailProvider

The dataprovider class "[EMAILPROVIDERCLASS]" could not be loaded.

Please contact your Telligent Evolution administrator. A technical explanation of why this error is caused is below.
Telligent Evolution expects to load a email provider class; in this case: [EMAILPROVIDERCLASS].

This class requires that certain methods and properties are implemented.

For more information you can also visit http://telligent.com the home of  Telligent Evolution.
